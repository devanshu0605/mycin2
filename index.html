About Us



Aritificial Intelligence is the future and the  FUTURE IS HERE!


The Mycin Innovation club came into existence on October 9th 2021 and the club is associated with the innovation centre of KIET Group of Institutions. We have Dr. Sushil Kumar , Dr. Ankit Kumar and Mr. Harsh Khatter as our mentors. The club moves forward under the leadership of president.

Our objective at Mycin is to educate everyone about the nuances of artificial intelligence & machine learning and how it is going to revolutionize  the future. 

We belive in learning via implementing vis-à-vis developing and deploying more and more projects.
